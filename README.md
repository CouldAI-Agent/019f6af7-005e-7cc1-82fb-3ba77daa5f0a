# Manhajar Gyara Bidiyo

Wannan manhaja ce ta fara gyara bidiyo wadda aka kirkira da [CouldAI](https://could.ai). Tana nuna yadda ake yin tsarin kewayawa na asali don manhajar gyara bidiyo a Flutter, kuma an tsara ta a harshen Hausa.

## Abubuwan da Manhajar ke yi

- Tana nuna shafin gida mai taken `Gyara Bidiyo`
- Tana da maballin zaben bidiyo don fara gyarawa
- An tsara ta da tsarin launi na Material Design (shu'udi)
- Tana amfani da tsarin hanyoyin (routes) na Flutter wajen farawa daga `/`

## Abubuwan da Aka Gina Da Su

- Flutter
- Dart
- Material Design

An kirkiri wannan manhaja ne ta hanyar CouldAI. Domin neman karin bayani, ziyarci [could.ai](https://could.ai).

## Tsarin Aiki

```text
lib/
  main.dart      Wurin fara manhajar, kewayawa, da shafin gyara bidiyo
pubspec.yaml     Bayanai akan fakiti da abubuwan da Flutter ke bukata
```

## Yadda Za'a Fara

Saka Flutter, sannan ka dauko abubuwan da manhajar ke bukata:

```bash
flutter pub get
```

Fara manhajar:

```bash
flutter run
```

## Game da CouldAI

An kirkiri wannan manhaja da [CouldAI](https://could.ai), maginin manhajojin AI don na'urori daban-daban. CouldAI na juyawa rubutun umarni zuwa cikakkun manhajoji na iOS, Android, Web, da Desktop tare da taimakon AI masu cin gashin kansu da ke tsara, gina, gwada, da kuma wallafa manhajoji a shirye.

Yi amfani da CouldAI a [could.ai](https://could.ai) don gina da gyara manhajojin na'urori daban-daban da taimakon AI.
